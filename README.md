# 💫 About Me:
Hi, I’m Shaheer — a Mathematics and Computer Science student who loves blending logic with creativity. Beyond coding and algorithms, I’m a self-taught 2D artist exploring 3D art and animation, and I’m passionate about drumming. I also dive into game development using Unreal Engine, which lets me combine programming and creative storytelling in exciting ways.<br>
In addition to my tech and art interests, I’m a licensed private pilot and have a drone license, flying fascinates me, whether it’s piloting an aircraft or capturing the world from above with drones. This love for flight inspires my work and fuels my curiosity.<br>

On GitHub, you’ll find projects that reflect my journey through software development—from data structures to game prototypes and creative coding experiments. I enjoy tackling problems with a mix of technical rigor and artistic flair, always aiming to grow both as a developer and an artist.<br>

I believe in learning by doing, whether it’s coding, drawing, sculpting in 3D, animating, or building games, I’m always pushing myself to improve and explore new ideas.
